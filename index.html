welcome to ladybug run

it sure is hot and sunny out today!

our poor ladybug is doomed if she can't find a shady spot to rest.

how to play

  • use the movement buttons (up, down, left & right) to lead our dear ladybug to that nice leaf.

  • once you have finished giving her movement instructions, press the run button and we'll see if she finds her way to safety!
